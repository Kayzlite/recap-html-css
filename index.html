What you will be learning

Soft Skills

Employers are looking for plant growers today who are capable of
solving problems in an intelligent and creative way. That is why we
at BeCode mainly learn to learn, work in a team, manage a project
well from A to Z.

Tech Skills

At BeCode you will discover all aspects of building a website or
mobile application. The course deals with both "Front-End" and
"Back-End" development. The Front-End, that is where the elements are
built that you see on the screen and with which you can interact.
The Back-End is the internal structure of the website or app.
